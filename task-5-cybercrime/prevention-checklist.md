## Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

1. **UPI Pin Safety:** Don't enter your UPI PIN for receiving money. Use it only for sending money.
2. **Verify UPI ID:** Always check the name on the app before hitting the 'Pay' button for the correct recipient.
3. **Strong Passwords:** Use unique passwords for university portals. Don't use your name or '12345' as the password.
4. **Enable 2FA:** Activate Two-Factor Authentication for WhatsApp, Instagram, and bank apps.
5. **Public Wi-Fi:** Don't access your bank accounts or university student portal on free Wi-Fi at the railway station or cafe.
6. **URL:** Only enter your data on websites starting with 'https://.' Check the spelling of the university website.
7. **Smishing:** Don't fall for the alert about your SIM card or electricity supply being blocked unless you click on the link.
8. **Privacy:** Make sure your social media profiles are private to avoid the risk of 'catfishing' or identity theft.

### Reporting Channels

In the event you fall prey or suffer financial loss, you can report the incident immediately to the following:

* **Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* **Helpline:** **1930** (National Cyber Crime Helpline)
