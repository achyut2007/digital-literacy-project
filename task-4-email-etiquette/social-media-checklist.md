# # Social Media Checklist

### Do's and Don'ts of Social Media for College Students



### ✅ Do's

- **Build Professional Identity** : Curate your LinkedIn.

- **Verify Source Credibility** : Fact-check before sharing.

- **Enable Multi-Factor Auth** : Secure all accounts.

- **Back Up Data** : Use cloud and local.

- **Network with Intent** : Connect with researchers.

- **Practice Digital Etiquette** : Be clear and concise.

### ❌ Don'ts

- **Avoid Public Venting** : Stay professional online.

- **Ignore Privacy Settings** : Audit your permissions.

- **Share Sensitive Data** : Protect personal information.

- **Plagiarize Digital Content** : Always attribute sources.

- **Neglect Screen Time** : Take regular breaks.

- **Click Unknown Links** : Avoid phishing traps.
